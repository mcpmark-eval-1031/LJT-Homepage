SynLogic is a framework for generating large-scale logical reasoning data with verifiable correctness. It synthesizes reasoning chains that can be programmatically verified, enabling the training of LLMs with reliable reasoning capabilities.

The key contributions include:
- A novel approach to generating verifiable logical reasoning data at scale
- Demonstration of improved reasoning performance on multiple benchmarks
- Open-source framework for the research community
