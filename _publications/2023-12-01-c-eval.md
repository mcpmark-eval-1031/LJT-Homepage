**Authors:** Yuzhen Huang, Yuzhuo Bai, Zhihao Zhu, Junlei Zhang, Jinghan Zhang, Tangjun Su, Junteng Liu, Chuancheng Lv, Yikai Zhang, Jiayi Lei, Yao Fu, Maosong Sun, Junxian He

**Venue:** NeurIPS 2023

C-Eval is the first comprehensive Chinese evaluation benchmark for foundation models, covering multiple disciplines and difficulty levels. It provides a rigorous assessment of foundation models' capabilities in the Chinese language across various academic domains.
