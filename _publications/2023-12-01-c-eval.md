C-Eval is a comprehensive multi-level multi-discipline Chinese evaluation suite for assessing foundation models. It covers multiple difficulty levels and subject areas, providing a thorough evaluation of model capabilities in Chinese.

Key features:
- Multi-level evaluation (middle school, high school, college, graduate)
- Multi-discipline coverage (STEM, humanities, social sciences)
- Large-scale benchmark with thousands of questions
