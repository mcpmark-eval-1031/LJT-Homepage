This paper discovers a universal truthfulness hyperplane inside large language models. We demonstrate that LLMs have an internal mechanism that separates truthful from hallucinated outputs, and we propose methods to detect and mitigate hallucinations by leveraging this internal structure.

Key contributions:
- Discovery of a universal truthfulness hyperplane in LLM representations
- Methods for detecting hallucinations using internal model states
- Approaches for steering models toward truthful outputs

**Authors**: Junteng Liu*, Shiqi Chen, Yu Cheng, Junxian He
