This paper investigates the perception bottleneck in vision-language models (VLMs) for chart understanding. We analyze how current VLMs process chart images and identify key limitations in their perception capabilities.

Key findings include:
- Identification of specific perception limitations in current VLMs for chart understanding
- Analysis of the bottlenecks that prevent accurate chart comprehension
- Insights for improving VLM architecture for visual reasoning tasks
