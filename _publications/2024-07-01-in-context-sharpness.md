This paper takes an inner representation perspective on hallucination mitigation in LLMs. We propose using in-context sharpness as an alert mechanism for detecting when models are likely to produce hallucinations.

Key contributions:
- Novel perspective on hallucination detection using inner representations
- In-context sharpness as an early warning signal
- Effective hallucination mitigation strategies

**Authors**: Shiqi Chen, Miao Xiong, Junteng Liu, Zhengxuan Wu, Teng Xiao, Siyang Gao, Junxian He
